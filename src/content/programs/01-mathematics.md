We use AI to turn abstract maths into something children can see and
interrogate. Students build and inspect a simple model that fits a line to
data, making slope, gradient, and optimisation tangible rather than
theoretical. They use an AI tutor to get step-by-step, level-matched
explanations on demand — the one-to-one help that usually costs money — and we
teach them to check the AI's working, so they learn the maths by catching its
mistakes.
